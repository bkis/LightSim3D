[SPACE] toggle this controls display

[UP]    increase sphere shininess
[DOWN]  decrease sphere shininess

[A]     toggle ambient light
[L]     toggle directional light
[S]     toggle shadows
[M]     cycle shadow mode

[C]     toggle auto camera movement
[LEFT]  turn camera left
[RIGHT] turn camera right
[P]     toggle parallel projection

[F1]    toggle FPS display
[F2]    toggle stats display
